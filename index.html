The Program

Built for Leaders Who Must Deliver Results

The Process Intelligence Academy is Africa's first professional certification program that fuses Lean Six Sigma methodology with practical AI integration — taught by one of the rarest-credentialed process experts on the continent.

This is not a theory course. Every module connects directly to the operational challenges facing Nigerian financial institutions and telecoms companies today — from customer churn and process waste to regulatory compliance and data quality.

Participants leave with two formal credentials, a live improvement project, and the ability to lead AI-augmented process transformation in their organisations.

8-Week Curriculum

From DMAIC Foundations to AI-Augmented Transformation

Week 01 · Sat–Sun

Foundations & AI Landscape

  • LSS History, Philosophy & DMAIC Overview
  • Nigeria's AI & Process Maturity Context
  • Intro to AI Tools for Process Professionals
  • Team Formation & Project Scoping
Week 02 · Sat–Sun

Define Phase + AI Requirements

  • Voice of the Customer (VOC) Techniques
  • SIPOC, CTQ Trees, Project Charter
  • AI-Assisted Requirements Gathering
  • Stakeholder Mapping with LLM Support
Week 03 · Sat–Sun

Measure Phase + Data Intelligence

  • Measurement System Analysis (MSA)
  • Process Capability (Cp, Cpk, Sigma Level)
  • AI-Powered Data Collection & Cleaning
  • Automated Dashboards & KPI Frameworks
Week 04 · Sat–Sun

Analyze Phase + AI Analytics

  • Root Cause Analysis (Fishbone, 5 Whys)
  • Statistical Analysis & Hypothesis Testing
  • Predictive Analytics with AI Models
  • Pattern Recognition & Anomaly Detection
Week 05 · Sat–Sun

Improve Phase + AI Automation

  • Solution Generation & FMEA
  • Design of Experiments (DOE)
  • Process Automation with AI Tools
  • Robotic Process Automation (RPA) Overview
Week 06 · Sat–Sun

Control Phase + AI Monitoring

  • Statistical Process Control (SPC)
  • Control Plans & Handover Frameworks
  • AI-Powered Real-Time Monitoring
  • Sustaining Gains & Change Management
Week 07 · Sat–Sun

Industry Deep Dive

  • Financial Services: KYC, Credit, Fraud Reduction
  • Telecoms: Churn, QoS, Revenue Assurance
  • Regulatory Compliance Integration
  • Nigerian Market Case Studies
Week 08 · Sat–Sun

Project Presentations & Certification

  • Live Project Presentations to Panel
  • Financial Impact Quantification
  • Certification Ceremony
  • Alumni Network & Continuing Education
